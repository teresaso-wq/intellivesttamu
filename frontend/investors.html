PT

Paul Tudor Jones

Founder, Tudor Investment Corp

Hedge Funds

Strategy: Global Macro + Technical Analysis · Net worth (approx.): ~$8B

📊 Public interviews + 13F filings

One of the most famous macro traders — known for navigating the 1987 Black Monday crash and for emphasizing risk management, gold, and Bitcoin as inflation hedges.

Strategy

  • Technical analysis — charts tell the story
  • Never average down on losing trades
  • Risk management above everything else
  • Gold and Bitcoin as inflation stores of value
  • Target 5:1 reward-to-risk on trades

“The most important rule of trading is to play great defense, not great offense.”

Recent Known Positions (2024)

  • Gold (GLD/futures) — large long
  • Bitcoin (BTC) — ~5% of portfolio (interviews)
  • S&P futures — tactical trades
  • Inflation hedges — TIPS, commodities

Famous Trade

Oct 1987 — Shorted S&P 500 before Black Monday · ~+200% during crash
